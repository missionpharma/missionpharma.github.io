Who is Brava Pharma Sales?

Brava Pharma Sales is a division of Mission Pharmacal Dermatology dedicated to delivering exceptional prescription products and meeting the needs of both healthcare providers and their patients. Brava Pharma Sales through acquisition and development brings to market two highly recognized branded drugs. The two brands, Plexion® and Keralac®, have significant brand loyalty and are formulated to provide topical treatment options to dermatologists and their patients with skin conditions such as acne, rosacea, dry rough skin and eczema. Brava Pharma Sales is committed to providing exceptional products and affordable access to meet the needs of its patients.
